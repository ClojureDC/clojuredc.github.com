ClojureDC

A friendly user group for the downtown DC area.

Join us to talk about, learn about, and hack around with an awesome functional programming language on the JVM, Clojure. Our goal is to have openness, accessibility, and fun for all levels of experience.

We meet every third Tuesday of the month. RSVP to attend on Meetup, chat in our Google Group, or follow us on Twitter.

The only prerequisites are an interest in building your skills in and furthering your understanding of Clojure, and willingness to encourage and enable your peers to do the same.

Thanks to LivingSocial for providing meeting space and support.

TIPS
  1. Don't be afraid, everyone is nervous about public speaking but we are nice people :)
  2. Practice beforehand. Has a shocking success rate (and I always forget this to my own detriment).
  3. Don't live code. Seriously, it can only not go poorly and there are too many easy screencast tools out there (or just keynote animations) for it not to be prerecorded.
  4. Pictures can say a lot more, with less explanation, then a block of text (or code)
If you have any tips for aspiring speakers, send them to our Google Group! Chris & Matt
